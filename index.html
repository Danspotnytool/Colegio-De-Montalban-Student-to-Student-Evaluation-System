Introduction

Embrace a new era of supportive learning with the "Colegio de Montalban Student to Student Evaluation System." Empower yourself and your peers to thrive academically while building a strong and supportive learning community.

Peer-to-Peer Evaluation

Our system facilitates constructive peer-to-peer evaluations, allowing students to provide feedback on their schoolmates' performance. This promotes a culture of mutual support and helps students recognize and appreciate each other's strengths.

User-Friendly Interface

Navigating the system is a breeze with its intuitive and user-friendly interface. Students can easily submit evaluations, view feedback, and track their progress, fostering a seamless experience that enhances engagement.

Confidentiality and Privacy

We prioritize the confidentiality of evaluations to create a safe space for honest feedback. The system ensures that the identity of evaluators remains confidential, promoting open communication without fear of judgment.

Feedback Moderation

To ensure the quality and relevance of feedback, the system incorporates moderation features. Faculty members may review and provide guidance on evaluations, ensuring that the feedback aligns with the college's values and educational objectives.
